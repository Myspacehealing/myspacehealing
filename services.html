Services & Conditions

Holistic care tailored to your needs.

Treatments are always individualized. Eva combines clinical experience with a gentle, intuitive approach to support both symptom relief and deeper long-term balance.

Services Offered

Acupuncture

Personalized treatment to support pain relief, nervous system regulation, healing, and overall balance.

Medical massage

Hands-on support for pain patterns, muscular tension, and restricted movement.

Therapeutic soft tissue manipulation

Manual techniques to improve mobility, release tension, and support recovery.

Cupping & Gua Sha

Traditional techniques to promote circulation, movement, and relief of stagnation and tightness.

Herbal consultation

Traditional Chinese Medicine guidance tailored to your presentation and goals.

Trigger point acupuncture

Focused work for tension patterns, pain, and stubborn musculoskeletal areas.

Facial & neurological acupuncture

Supportive care for the head and facial region, addressing issues such as facial paralysis, nerve irritation, numbness, twitching, sinus congestion, tinnitus, headaches, and neurological imbalances.

Two-person home visits

Special pricing may be available when two people receive treatment during the same home visit.
