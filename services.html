Why In-Home Treatment?

Your healing space should feel like yours. That’s why Eva comes to you.

Your space, your comfort

Play your own music. Watch your favorite show. Light a candle. Wrap up in your own blanket. You set the atmosphere — Eva brings the care. There’s no sterile waiting room, no fluorescent lights, no rushing out the door when it’s over. You’re already home.

Stay present in your life

Take a work call before your session. Keep an eye on your kids. Be near your family, your pets, your routines. In-home treatment fits around your life instead of interrupting it — especially valuable for busy professionals, new parents, and caregivers who can’t easily step away.

Rest and recover immediately

After a deeply relaxing session, the last thing you want is a subway ride or a drive through traffic. At home, you can close your eyes, take a nap, or simply sit with the stillness. The benefits of treatment deepen when your body can settle without rushing to the next thing.

Privacy and personal attention

No shared treatment rooms, no curtain dividers, no strangers nearby. In-home sessions are completely private — just you and Eva. This allows for unhurried conversation, a deeper therapeutic relationship, and the freedom to fully let go.

Ideal for limited mobility

For patients recovering from surgery, managing chronic pain, in postpartum recovery, or living with conditions that make travel difficult — in-home care removes the biggest barrier to getting treatment. Eva brings everything needed directly to your door.

Surrounded by people you love

Some patients find comfort in having a partner, parent, or friend nearby during treatment. In-home visits make that natural. Your support system is already there — and with two-person visit pricing, a loved one can even receive treatment during the same session.
