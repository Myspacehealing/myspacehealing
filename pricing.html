Pricing & Important Policies

Professional, straightforward, and fair.

Everything you need to know about pricing, appointments, policies, and what to expect from your visit.

How much does a visit cost?

Private in-home acupuncture sessions begin at $250.

When two people receive treatment during the same visit, combined sessions begin at $350. This option is often popular for partners, family members, or friends who wish to receive care together. Two-person visits require both patients to be treated within the same appointment window.

Each home visit is scheduled for approximately 75–90 minutes, which includes consultation, treatment, setup, and cleanup. The primary acupuncture treatment itself typically lasts about 60 minutes from needling to completion.

Additional Traditional Chinese Medicine techniques may be incorporated depending on the patient's needs.

Available add-on therapies

Cupping?Plastic cups create gentle suction on the skin to improve blood flow, ease muscle tension, and reduce inflammation. Adds roughly 10–15 minutes to your visit. — $40
Gua sha?A smooth-edged tool is used in short strokes along the skin to boost circulation, release fascial tension, and support the body’s natural healing response. Adds roughly 10–15 minutes to your visit. — $40
Moxibustion?An incense made from dried mugwort is burned close to the skin or acupuncture points to stimulate circulation, warm the meridians, and support immune function. Adds roughly 10–15 minutes to your visit. — $40
Ear seeds?Tiny seeds or metal beads are placed on specific points of the ear with adhesive tape, providing gentle acupressure that continues working between visits. Adds roughly 5 minutes to your visit. — $10
Therapeutic bodywork / medical massage?Targeted hands-on manual therapy focusing on pain relief, muscle recovery, and tissue mobilization. Available in 10-minute increments up to 60 minutes. Only available as part of an acupuncture visit, not offered as a standalone appointment. — $30 per additional 10 minutes

Add-on services may be discussed prior to the appointment or during the consultation. If an add-on therapy is planned and prepaid in advance but ultimately not provided during the treatment, the prepaid amount for that service will be refunded. When add-on therapies are incorporated, portions of the acupuncture treatment time may be adjusted accordingly within the scheduled visit window.

Appointment hours

Standard appointment hours are 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM.

Appointments scheduled after 7:00 PM are subject to an additional $50 evening fee, and the latest available appointment time is 9:00 PM.

Holiday visits

Visits requested on holidays are subject to an additional $100 holiday fee.

Observed holidays may include: New Year's Eve, New Year's Day, Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, Chinese New Year (Lunar New Year Day), and Mid-Autumn Festival. Holiday availability may vary depending on scheduling.

Group & Corporate

Group wellness, corporate events & retreats

In addition to private home visits, Eva may also provide acupuncture services for corporate wellness programs, small wellness gatherings, retreats and healing events, and group wellness experiences.

These services are customized based on the number of participants, duration of the event, and travel requirements.

Please email eva@myspacehealing.com with the details of the event, including: number of participants, estimated hours, event location, and proposed budget. Providing this information helps ensure the event can be arranged efficiently.

Pricing adjustments

Pricing may vary depending on travel distance, parking/tolls/travel time, number of participants, and scheduling considerations. For visits outside of New York City, additional arrangements may apply. Final pricing is always confirmed prior to scheduling.

Payment & prepayment policy

Payment is accepted via Zelle, Venmo, or cash.

All treatments must be prepaid at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled appointment in order to confirm the visit.

Appointments are confirmed only after completion of the intake form, address verification, and prepayment for the visit. This helps ensure scheduling availability and proper travel planning.

Cancellation policy

Appointments require at least 24 hours notice for cancellation or rescheduling.

Because treatment time is reserved specifically for each patient and travel arrangements are made in advance:

  • Cancellations with at least 24 hours notice may receive a full refund or apply the prepaid amount toward a future appointment
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of the scheduled appointment may not be eligible for a refund
  • Missed appointments are non-refundable

Travel & scheduling delays

While every effort is made to arrive on time, travel delays caused by traffic, weather, or other unforeseen circumstances may occasionally occur. If delays arise, Eva will communicate updates as soon as possible.

If an appointment must be rescheduled due to travel delays or unforeseen circumstances, the prepaid amount may be applied to a rescheduled appointment or refunded if rescheduling is not possible.

Refund policy

Payments for services already rendered are non-refundable. If a prepaid add-on service is planned and paid for but ultimately not provided during the visit, the prepaid amount for that service will be refunded.

Appointments canceled with proper notice (24 hours or more) may receive a refund or be applied toward a future appointment. Appointments canceled due to unsafe conditions, illegal activity, or inappropriate behavior will not be refunded.

Where are visits available?

Visits are primarily available throughout New York City. Travel to New Jersey, Connecticut, or upstate New York may be arranged depending on scheduling availability and travel considerations.

Right to refuse service

Eva reserves the right to decline or discontinue treatment if circumstances are unsafe, inappropriate, illegal, or outside the scope of professional practice. If a visit must be canceled or terminated due to misconduct, illegal activity, or unsafe conditions, the prepaid amount will not be refunded.

What is required for a treatment?

Clients should provide:

  • A clean and quiet space suitable for treatment
  • Enough space for a person to sit comfortably with support for the back, head, arms, and legs (such as a lounge chair) — or space for individuals to lie flat comfortably during treatment
  • A comfortable indoor temperature

Safety policy

For personal safety and professional boundaries:

  • Appointments must be scheduled in advance
  • The environment must be safe and appropriate for treatment
  • Pets should be secured so they do not interfere with treatment

Eva reserves the right to decline or discontinue a visit if conditions are unsafe or inappropriate.

Illness policy

If you are experiencing a contagious illness, including fever, flu symptoms, COVID, chickenpox, or other airborne infections, please reschedule your appointment. This helps protect both practitioner and patients.

Professional boundaries

Eva maintains professional clinical boundaries during all visits. Any inappropriate behavior will result in immediate termination of the session.

Treatment of minors

Patients under the age of 18 must have a parent or legal guardian present to provide consent for treatment.

Who These Visits Are Best For

Private acupuncture visits are especially helpful for busy professionals, postpartum mothers, fertility and IVF patients, individuals experiencing pain or mobility limitations, and those who value privacy and personalized care.

Services may also be arranged for corporate wellness programs, small wellness gatherings, retreats, and group healing events.
