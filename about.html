About Eva

Experienced, calm, and deeply personalized care.

(Eva) Ching Po Huang is a New York State licensed acupuncturist and certified herbologist with over 13 years of clinical experience providing Traditional Chinese Medicine–based care for a wide range of conditions.

Professional background

Eva graduated from Pacific College in 2012 and began her career working in several acupuncture practices shortly after graduation. Those early experiences gave her important clinical exposure, but they also made something very clear to her: many of those settings did not allow her to care for patients in the way she believed they truly needed. Treatment sessions were often limited to less than 30 minutes, and care was frequently restricted to only one or two areas of concern, even when patients were dealing with broader, more complex patterns in their health. Eva found this deeply misaligned with her values and with her understanding of what real healing requires.

She believed patients deserved more time, more attention, and more thoughtful, individualized care. Rather than offering rushed treatments within rigid limitations, Eva wanted to fully assess each person, understand the root of their concerns, and support them as a whole human being. That conviction led her to establish Yupo Wellness in Tribeca in 2013, where she dedicated herself to providing care with greater depth, presence, and integrity.

In 2018, Eva expanded her work by founding Yupo Health in Midtown East. Since then, she has continued to care for patients with deep commitment, compassion, and professional passion. Even with a busy practice, Eva continues to learn, study, and grow. She believes a practitioner should never stop expanding their knowledge, because every patient deserves the very best care she can offer.

Philosophy of care

Eva approaches each patient with sincerity, respect, and a strong sense of purpose. She sees every person who comes to her as someone placed in her path for a reason, and she considers it part of her mission to help them with the greatest care, skill, and heart that she can give.

Over the years, Eva has helped many patients regain their health, avoid surgeries, restore balance, improve quality of life, and conceive long-awaited babies. Many of her patients continue to return to her because they trust not only her knowledge and skill, but also the way she treats them — with decency, warmth, love, and genuine care.

The birth of MySpace Healing

Some time ago, Eva began to feel a strong inner calling that there were people beyond the clinic who needed her help. She listened to that voice, and from that calling, MySpace Healing was born — a more personal offering that allows her to bring healing directly to those who need it, in the comfort of their own space.

In a way, if you have found her here, perhaps this was meant to be part of your healing journey too.

Why home visits matter

  • Greater privacy and comfort
  • Helpful for people with limited mobility or pain
  • Ideal for busy professionals
  • Supportive for postpartum recovery
  • A quieter, more personalized treatment environment

Areas served

Home visits are primarily available throughout New York City. Travel to New Jersey, Connecticut, upstate New York, or other locations may be arranged depending on scheduling availability and travel considerations.
