About Eva

Experienced, calm, and deeply personalized care.

Eva Huang is a New York State licensed acupuncturist and certified herbologist with over 13 years of clinical experience providing Traditional Chinese Medicine–based care for a wide range of conditions.

Professional background

Eva supports patients with fertility and IVF care, hormonal imbalance, chronic pain, musculoskeletal injuries, headaches, anxiety, stress-related disorders, sleep issues, chronic fatigue, and many other concerns.

She provides comprehensive patient assessments and develops individualized treatment plans that may integrate acupuncture, herbal medicine, bodywork, and practical lifestyle guidance.

Philosophy of care

Eva's approach focuses on identifying underlying patterns rather than only addressing symptoms. The goal is to help the body regulate, recover, and return to better balance over time.

"A calm body and a calm mind create the foundation for deeper healing."

Why home visits matter

  • Greater privacy and comfort
  • Helpful for people with limited mobility or pain
  • Ideal for busy professionals
  • Supportive for postpartum recovery
  • A quieter, more personalized treatment environment

Areas served

Home visits are primarily available throughout New York City. Travel to upstate New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, or other locations may be possible depending on scheduling and compensation.
