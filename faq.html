FAQ & Policies

Simple, clear, and professional.

These are suggested policies for a concierge-style home acupuncture practice and can be adjusted any time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a home visit cost?
Private in-home sessions start at $250 and increase based on travel distance and visit details.
Do you offer two-person visits?
Yes. Special combined pricing may be available when two people receive treatment during the same home visit.
What payment methods do you accept?
Suggested methods: cash, Zelle, and Venmo. You can add card payments later if you want.
Do new patients need to prepay?
For concierge care, prepayment for first visits is a common and sensible policy.
What is your cancellation policy?
At least 24 hours notice is recommended for cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or missed appointments may be charged the full visit fee.

Recommended website language

Payment methods: Cash, Zelle, and Venmo accepted.

Prepayment: New patients may be asked to prepay in order to reserve an appointment time.

Cancellation policy: Appointments require at least 24 hours notice for cancellation or rescheduling. Late cancellations or missed appointments may be charged the full session fee.

Travel policy: Home visits are primarily available throughout New York City, with travel to other areas available by arrangement.

A practical way many solo practitioners handle concierge appointments is to collect intake information before the first visit, request prepayment for new patients, and keep payment options simple with cash, Zelle, and Venmo.
