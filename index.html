Private Concierge Acupuncture

Healing, balance, and personalized care in the comfort of your space.

MySpace Healing offers private in-home acupuncture and holistic care with Ching Po (Eva) Huang, a New York State licensed acupuncturist and certified herbologist with over 13 years of clinical experience.

Care is designed to reduce pain, calm the nervous system, support fertility and hormonal balance, and create space for deeper healing in a quiet, personalized setting.
